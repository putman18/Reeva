# LinkedIn Posting Directive - The Finance Blueprint

## Goal
Drive traffic from LinkedIn to thefinanceblueprint.com by posting personal finance content 3x per week under Jordan Hale's personal profile.

## What Works in 2026 (Research-Backed)
- Personal profiles get 8x more engagement than company pages - always post as Jordan, not the brand
- Document posts (carousels) get 6.6% engagement rate - highest of any format
- Short native videos (30-90 seconds) growing 2x faster than other formats
- Posts with external links get 40% less reach - put the link in the first comment, not the post
- Comments count 2x more than likes - reply to every comment within the first hour
- Best posting times: Tuesday/Wednesday 8-10 AM or 12-2 PM local time
- Post 3-5x per week for best results

## Post Formats to Rotate

### Format 1: Hook + List (text only)
```
[Bold opening line that stops the scroll]

Most people don't know this about [topic]:

1. [Point]
2. [Point]  
3. [Point]
4. [Point]
5. [Point]

[Closing insight]

Full breakdown on my site (link in comments)
```

### Format 2: Story + Lesson
```
I [did something] and it [result].

Here's what I learned:

[2-3 paragraph story]

The lesson: [one sentence]

[CTA - link in comments]
```

### Format 3: Myth vs Reality
```
Everyone says [common advice].

Here's why that's wrong:

[Myth]: [explanation]
[Reality]: [better approach]

[Repeat 3-4 times]

Save this for later.
```

## Content Calendar
- Monday: Skip (lower engagement)
- Tuesday: List post (tips, mistakes, facts)
- Wednesday: Story post (personal finance scenario)
- Thursday: Myth vs Reality or data post
- Friday: Skip or engagement post (question to audience)

## Rules
- Never put article links in the post body - always first comment
- Always end with a question or CTA
- Reply to every comment within 60 minutes of posting
- Use 3-5 relevant hashtags: #PersonalFinance #MoneyTips #FinancialFreedom #Investing #DebtFree
- Tag relevant people only if genuinely relevant (not spam)
