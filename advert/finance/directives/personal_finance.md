side hustles, debt payoff, and passive income. Angle: practical, no-fluff advice written
like a smart friend explaining it, not a bank.

## Article Format

1. Hook - relatable opening (cost of doing nothing / missing out)
2. TL;DR / Quick answer (captures featured snippet position)
3. Main content with H2 subheadings
4. Comparison table for tool/product roundups
5. FAQ section (5-8 questions)
6. Conclusion with clear next step CTA

Tone: conversational, direct, no jargon without explanation. Like texting a friend who
happens to know a lot about money.

## Publishing Rules
- 3-5 articles per day
- Minimum 1,500 words, target 2,000
- One primary keyword per article
- Every article gets 1-2 affiliate links placed naturally in context
- No fake "expert" claims - write from the perspective of someone who researched it

## KPIs
- Articles published per week (target: 20-30)
- Total keywords covered
- Affiliate clicks and signups
- Ad revenue once traffic hits 10k/month (apply to Mediavine at 50k sessions)
